What are the specific torque requirements and seal replacement intervals for the CX-900 High-Pressure Turbine assembly?

14:02 PM
Confidence: High link Citations: [1], [2]
smart_toy

Based on the technical manual for the CX-900 High-Pressure Turbine, the following specifications are mandatory for safe operation:

Torque Requirements

  • Main Flange Bolts: 1,250 Nm ± 5% (Grade 12.9 hardware only)
  • Access Port Covers: 450 Nm cross-pattern sequence
  • Bleed Valve Interface: 85 Nm with anti-seize compound [1]

Seal Replacement Intervals

Standard replacement is required every 12,000 operational hours. However, the PTFE Primary Seal (Part #99-XJ) must be inspected during every minor maintenance cycle (4,000 hours) [2].
